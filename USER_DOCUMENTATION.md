## Welcome to BachatKaro {#welcome}

**BachatKaro** ("Save Money" in Hindi) is an intelligent expense tracking application designed for Indian users who want to save money, manage shared expenses, and plan group trips — all in one place.

### What Problems Does BachatKaro Solve?

- 💸 **Complicated Bill Splitting**: Forget confusing calculations. BachatKaro automatically splits bills among group members
- 🤝 **Group Expense Confusion**: Track who paid what and who owes whom instantly
- 🗺️ **Trip Planning Stress**: Generate smart trip recommendations with budgets and local tips
- 🎯 **Savings Confusion**: Set goals and track your progress toward financial targets
- 💰 **Hidden Spending**: Understand your spending patterns with detailed analytics

### Who Is BachatKaro For?

- College students managing shared expenses
- Friend groups planning trips together
- Families splitting household costs
- Roommates dividing rent and utilities
- Anyone who wants to track and reduce spending

## Getting Started {#getting-started}

1. **Open BachatKaro** in your web browser
2. **Click "Sign Up"** on the login screen
3. **Enter your details**:

   - Confirm password
4. **Click "Create Account"**

1. **Enter your email** and password
2. **Click "Sign In"**
3. **You'll see the Dashboard** with all features available

## Managing Expenses {#managing-expenses}

### Adding a Single Expense - 3 Methods

#### Method 1: Manual Entry (Classic Way)

1. **From Dashboard**: Click **"➕ Add Expense"** button
2. **Fill the form**:
   - **Amount**: Enter the expense amount
   - **Category**: Choose from Food, Shopping, Bills, Travel, Others
   - **Payment Mode**: UPI, Cash, Net Banking, or Card
   - **Date**: Pick the date (today by default)
   - **Notes**: Describe what you bought (e.g., "Dinner at Dominoes")
3. **Click "Save Expense"**
4. **Confirmation**: You'll see "✓ Expense Added!" toast

#### Method 2: Voice Input (Fast & Smart)

**Perfect for**: When you're busy, driving, or hands are full

1. **Look for the red microphone button** 🎤 (bottom-right on mobile, top-right on desktop)
2. **Click the mic button** — it turns red and starts listening
3. **Say your expense in Hindi or English**:
   - English: "500 for petrol" or "200 rupees for coffee"
   - Hindi: "500 का पेट्रोल" or "200 का डिनर"
   - Hinglish: "250 ka chai" or "1000 ki shopping"
4. **BachatKaro automatically**:
   - Extracts the amount
   - Guesses the category (Food, Travel, Shopping, Bills)
   - Saves within 1 second
5. **You hear**: "₹500 Added! ✅"

**Voice Tips**:
- Say the **amount first**: "500 for food" (not "food for 500")
- Use rupees/amount words: "250 rupees" or "2 sо"
- Category detection is automatic from keywords:
  - **Food**: khana, chai, dinner, restaurant, pizza, dosa
  - **Travel**: petrol, uber, safari, bus, train
  - **Shopping**: kirana, clothes, amazon, mall, shopping
  - **Bills**: rent, electricity, water, recharge, wifi
  - **Others**: anything else goes to "Others"

#### Method 3: Quick Add (Dashboard)

1. **On the Dashboard, find "Recent Expenses"** section
2. **Click icon next to date to add quickly** (some browsers allow this)
3. **Shortcut for mobile**: Use voice input for fastest entry

### Viewing & Organizing Expenses

**On the Dashboard**:
- **Recent Expenses**: Last 5 expenses shown
- **Category Chart**: Visual breakdown of spending by category
- **Filter by Date**:
  - This Month (default)
  - This Week
  - Custom date range
- **Monthly Trend**: See if you're spending more/less this month

### Editing an Expense

1. **Find the expense** in Recent Expenses or scroll down
2. **Click the pencil icon** 📝 
3. **Edit any field**: Amount, category, date, notes
4. **Click "Save Changes"**
5. **The change updates** instantly

### Deleting an Expense

1. **Find the expense** in the list
2. **Click the trash icon** 🗑️
3. **Confirm deletion** when prompted
4. **Expense is removed** (you can't undo this, so be careful!)

## Group Expenses & Bill Splitting {#group-expenses}

### Why Use Groups?

When you share expenses with others — roommates, friends on a trip, family events — BachatKaro **automatically splits the bill** and tracks who owes whom.

### Creating a Group

1. **Go to Dashboard → Groups tab**
2. **Click "➕ Create Groups"** button
3. **Fill the form**:
   - **Group Name**: "College Roommates" or "Trip to Goa 2025"
   - **Members**: Add names of people in the group
   - **Click "Create"**
4. **You're the Admin** — you can add/remove members and expenses

### Joining a Group

**Someone invited you to a group?**

1. **You'll see a notification** or receive an invite code
2. **Click "Join Groups"** on the dashboard
3. **Paste the invite code** they sent you
4. **Click "Join"**
5. **You're now part of the group** and can see all expenses

### Adding a Group Expense

1. **From Groups Tab**: Select the group
2. **Click "Add Expense"**
3. **Fill details**:
   - **Amount**: Total bill amount
   - **Who paid**: Select from members (usually you)
   - **Category**: Food, Travel, etc.
   - **Members involved**: Select who should share this cost
4. **Click "Split Equally"** (each person pays equally) or customize splits
5. **Final screen shows**: Who owes whom how much

**Example**: 
> You paid ₹3000 for a restaurant bill with 3 friends. App splits: Everyone owes ₹750 each. If you paid, you'll be reimbursed ₹2250 total.

### Viewing Group Settlements

**Who owes who?**

1. **In the Group view**, scroll to "Settlements" section
2. **You'll see**:
   - Arrows showing direction of payment
   - Amounts owed
   - Names clearly marked

**It shows**: 
- ✅ Green = You are owed money
- 🔴 Red = You owe money
- ⚪ Gray = Already settled

### Settling a Payment

1. **See someone owes you money**? Click "Mark as Settled" next to their name
2. **You owe someone**? Transfer them money on UPI, then click "Mark as Settled"
3. **Payment is recorded** and removed from the open list

### Removing a Member

**Admin only feature**:

1. **Go to the group**
2. **Find the member's name**
3. **Click the "X" or trash icon** next to their name
4. **Confirm removal**

> ⚠️ **Note**: Open debts with that member stay recorded until settled

## Trip Planning {#trip-planning}

### What Is Trip Planning?

Instead of manually researching destinations, BachatKaro generates **smart trip recommendations** including:
- Must-see places
- Hotel suggestions
- Local food recommendations
- Budget breakdown
- Travel tips in Hindi & English

### Generating a Trip Plan

1. **Go to Groups tab** → Select a group
2. **Click "Plan Trip"** button (🗺️ icon)
3. **A dialog opens for trip details**:
   - **Destination**: Where are you going? (e.g., "Jaipur", "Goa", "Kerala")
   - **Members**: How many people? (auto-filled from group)
   - **Budget per Person**: ₹5000? ₹10000? ₹20000?
4. **Click "✨ Generate Trip Plan"**
5. **Wait 2-3 seconds** while the app creates your plan

### Understanding Your Trip Plan

Once generated, you'll see:

#### 📍 **Places to Visit**
- Top attractions listed (Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace)
- Mix of famous and local experiences

#### 🏨 **Hotels**
- Budget, mid-range, and luxury options
- Real hotel names where possible

#### 🍽️ **Local Food**
- Must-try dishes native to that place
- Street food recommendations

#### 🚗 **Local Travel**
- How to get around (auto, taxi, buses, metro)
- Money-saving tips

#### 💰 **Budget Breakdown**
- How to spend your budget:
  - % for accommodation
  - % for food
  - % for travel
  - % for activities

#### 🧠 **Smart Advice**
- Money-saving hacks specific to that destination
- Local insider tips
- Best time to visit, crowd info, etc.

### Sharing the Trip Plan

See the perfect destination in your plan?

1. **At the bottom of the plan**, click **"Share on WhatsApp"** 📱
2. **WhatsApp opens** with a pre-written message
3. **Send to your group** or individual friends
4. **They can click the link** to view the plan themselves

**Or**:
- Click **"View on Maps"** to see the destination on Google Maps
- Click **"Copy Link"** to share via other apps

### Viewing Previously Generated Plans

1. **In the group**, trip plans are **saved automatically** 💾
2. **If you return to the group later**, the plan appears instantly
3. **You can generate a new plan** for a different destination

## Bill Roulette {#bill-roulette}

### What Is Bill Roulette?

**Bill Roulette** is a fun game where the **spinning wheel randomly picks who pays the bill** 🎡

Perfect for:
- Deciding who gets the check at a restaurant
- Fair way to pick who pays for snacks
- Making bill-paying fun instead of awkward

### Playing Bill Roulette

1. **In Groups tab**, click **"Bill Roulette"** button 🎡
2. **A spinning wheel appears** with all group members' names
3. **Click "Spin"** to start the wheel
4. **Wheel spins for 3 seconds** with fun animations
5. **It slows down and stops** on a name
6. **That person pays the bill** 💸
7. **A funny message appears** (movie dialogues, Bollywood jokes)
8. **Everyone laughs** 😂

### Sharing the Result

1. **After the wheel stops**, click **"Share on WhatsApp"** 📱
2. **A message is created** with:
   - Winner's name
   - Funny quote
   - Link to BachatKaro
3. **Send it to your group** via WhatsApp
4. **Everyone sees** who got picked!

**Sample funny messages**:
- "Sadak se utha ke... Star bana diya (Bill Payer)! ⭐"
- "Aata Majhi Satakli... Bill dekh ke! 🤯"
- "Control Uday, Control! Paisa ja raha hai 🧘‍♂️"
- "Gulaal + Raees = Bill Wale (New Bollywood mashup) 🎬"

## Common Workflows {#workflows}

### Workflow 1: Planning a Group Trip

**You're planning a trip with friends. Here's the complete flow:**

1. **Create a Group** ("Goa Trip 2025") → Add all friends
2. **Collect money** from everyone for common expenses
3. **Add Group Expenses** as things are paid for:
   - "₹5000 for hotel" (you paid)
   - "₹3000 for car rental" (friend paid)
4. **Generate a Trip Plan** → Choose destination, budget
5. **Share the plan** on WhatsApp with the group
6. **Everyone reviews** the places, hotels, budget tips
7. **When trip ends**, settle all payments
8. **Save the memories** (group stay remembers all expenses!)

### Workflow 2: Splitting Monthly Rent & Utilities

**You share an apartment with 2 roommates:**

1. **Create Group** ("Apartment ABC")
2. **Add members** (Roommate 1, Roommate 2)
3. **Every month, add collective expenses**:
   - **Rent**: ₹30,000 (you paid) → Split 3 ways = ₹10,000 each
   - **Water Bill**: ₹1,500 → Split equally
   - **Electricity**: ₹3,500 → Split equally
   - **Internet**: ₹2,000 → Split equally
4. **System shows instantly**:
   - Who owes whom
   - How much total
5. **At month-end**, roommates pay you back
6. **Mark as settled** once payments received

### Workflow 3: Everyday Expense Tracking

**Just want to track your personal spending:**

1. **Use Dashboard → Home tab** for daily tracking
2. **Add expenses daily** (voice is fastest!)
3. **Every week, review** the trend chart
4. **Set a monthly budget** in Planning tab
5. **Use analytics** to find spending patterns
6. **Reduce** as needed

### Workflow 4: Saving for a Goal

**You want to save ₹100,000 for a laptop in 1 year:**

1. **Create Goal** in My Dreams tab
2. **Set ₹100,000 as target**, Dec 2025 as date
3. **Calculate**: ₹100,000 ÷ 12 months = ₹8,300 per month
4. **Set monthly savings to ₹8,500** (be generous!)
5. **Every month, log your savings** to the goal
6. **Track progress** on the visual bar
7. **Celebrate** when you reach 100%! 🎉

## Troubleshooting & FAQs {#troubleshooting}

### Q1: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

**A:** 
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email (including spam folder) for reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. You can now log in with the new password

---

### Q2: Voice input isn't working. What should I do?

**Troubleshooting steps**:

1. **Check browser compatibility**: Works best on Chrome, Edge, Safari
2. **Allow microphone permission**: When the browser asks for mic access, click "Allow"
3. **Speak clearly**: Avoid noisy environments
4. **Say the amount first**: "500 for food" (not "food for 500")
5. **Try again**: Reload the page and try voice input again
6. **Fallback**: Use manual entry if voice keeps failing

---

### Q3: Why doesn't my group show expenses I added?

**Possible causes**:

1. **Refresh the page**: Sometimes browser cache shows old data
2. **Check your internet**: Ensure you're online and connected
3. **Verify the group**: Make sure you added expense to the right group
4. **Check date filter**: Maybe the filter is hiding the expense date
5. **Logout and login again**: Clears cache and refreshes everything

---

### Q4: A friend is in my group but we haven't settled yet. Can I remove them?

**A:** Yes, but:

1. **Open the group**
2. **Click the person's name and click "Remove"**
3. **The system remembers** any money owed by/to them
4. **They stay in the records** until paid (for accounting)
5. **They can't access** the group anymore though

**Best practice**: Settle payment before removing someone.

---

### Q5: How accurate is the bill splitting calculation?

**A:** 100% accurate!

- **Equal splits**: Amount ÷ Number of people
- **Custom splits**: You define exactly who owes what
- **Automatic tracking**: System remembers running balances
- **Settlement tracking**: Who paid owed vs. whose balance is settled

No manual errors — the app does all math!

---

### Q6: Can I export my expense data?

**A:** Not yet in the current version, but you can:

1. **Screenshot** your Dashboard
2. **Export summary** from each month
3. **Print the browser page** for records
4. **Request future feature** — the team is always improving!

### Q7: What if I accidentally deleted an expense?

**A:** Currently, **deleted expenses can't be recovered**. So:

1. **Double-check before deleting**
2. **Use "Edit" if you made a mistake** in the amount/category
3. **Mark as settled** rather than deleting if it's old
4. **Contact support** if you deleted something critical

## Security & Privacy {#security}

### How Is My Data Protected?

- 🔐 **Encryption**: All data travels over HTTPS (encrypted connection)
- 🛡️ **Password Security**: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- 🔑 **Authentication**: Only you can access your data after login
- 🚫 **No Sharing**: Your personal expenses aren't visible to others

### What Data Do We Collect?

We collect only what's necessary:
- **Account**: Email, password (hashed), name
- **Expenses**: Amounts, categories, dates, notes
- **Groups**: Member names, expense records
- **Settings**: Language preference, theme

### What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Payment method details (unless you tell us)
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Browsing history outside BachatKaro

### Privacy Tips

1. **Don't share your password** with anyone
2. **Logout** when using shared computers
3. **Don't post screenshots** of sensitive expense details online
4. **Use strong passwords**: Mix of numbers, letters, symbols
5. **Clear browser cache** occasionally for privacy

### What About Group Expenses?

- **Group members can see**: All shared expenses, amounts, who paid
- **They can't see**: Your personal expenses or other groups
- **Admin can remove**: Members who leave the group

## Glossary {#glossary}

### **AutoSplit / Splitting**
Automatically dividing a bill equally among group members. BachatKaro does the math instantly.

### **Bakra** (Slang)
Person picked by Bill Roulette to pay the bill! 😂

### **Batch** (Financial)
A group of related expenses or transactions reviewed together.

### **Chai** (Hindi)
Tea. Common category in voice input ("₹20 ka chai").

### **Category**
Type of expense: Food, Shopping, Bills, Travel, Others.

### **Deep Link**
A special link that takes you directly to a trip plan, even if you're not logged in yet.

### **EMI** (Equated Monthly Installment)
Regular monthly payment for loans (car loan, phone loan, home loan, etc.).

### **Group**
Collection of people sharing expenses. Examples: roommates, vacation friends, sports team.

### **Hinglish**
Mixture of Hindi and English. BachatKaro voice understands Hinglish! "₹500 ka petrol" = "500 rupees for petrol"

### **Kirana** (Hindi)
Grocery shopping, household essentials.

### **Ledger**
Complete record of all expenses and who owes whom (the app maintains this automatically).

### **Payment Mode**
Method of payment: UPI, Cash, Net Banking, Card.

### **Petrol**
Fuel/gasoline for vehicles (common expense category).

### **Settlement**
Marking an expense as paid. When money changes hands and debt is cleared.

### **Settlements Panel**
View in the group showing "who owes whom" until all debts are settled.

### **Smart Advice**
AI-powered money-saving tips specific to your situation or destination.

### **Trip Plan**
AI-generated itinerary with places, hotels, food, travel tips, and budget for a destination.

### **UPI**
Unified Payments Interface (Indian digital payment system like Google Pay, PhonePe).

### **Web App**
BachatKaro version you access through browsers (like Chrome, Safari, Edge).
