# BachatKaro Quick Start Guide
## Get Started in 5 Minutes

---

## First Time? Start Here! ⚡

### 👤 Step 1: Create Your Account (30 seconds)

1. Go to **BachatKaro.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter email and password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. ✅ **Done!** You're in

---

### 💰 Step 2: Add Your First Expense (15 seconds)

**Option A - Voice (Fastest)**:
1. Click the **red mic 🎤** button
2. Say: **"500 for dinner"** or **"500 का खाना"**
3. Done! ✅

**Option B - Manual**:
1. Click **"Add Expense"**
2. Enter amount: 500
3. Pick category: Food
4. Click **"Save"**
5. Done! ✅

---

### 👥 Step 3: Create a Group for Friends (1 minute)

1. Go to **"Groups"** tab
2. Click **"Create Group"**
3. Name it: "Trip to Goa" or "Roommates"
4. Add friends' names
5. Click **"Create"**
6. ✅ Share the invite code with friends

---

### 🗺️ Step 4: Plan a Trip (2 minutes)

1. Select your group
2. Click **"Plan Trip"** button
3. Enter:
   - Where: "Goa" or "Jaipur"
   - Budget: "₹10,000" per person
4. Click **"✨ Generate Trip Plan"**
5. Wait 2 seconds
6. ✅ View places, hotels, food recommendations
7. Click **"Share on WhatsApp"**

---

### 🎡 Step 5: Play Bill Roulette (1 minute)

1. In the group, click **"Bill Roulette"**
2. Click **"Spin"** to spin the wheel
3. Wheel picks the lucky person! 🎁
4. Click **"Share on WhatsApp"** to send result
5. ✅ Everyone sees the funny message

---

## Common Tasks

### I want to add an expense right now
**Fastest way**: Click the **mic 🎤**, say "500 for coffee", done in 1 second!

### I want to split a restaurant bill with friends
1. **Create group** with friends
2. **Add expense** for total bill
3. **Select friends** who should pay
4. **App splits automatically** ✓

### I want to see who owes me money
1. Go to your **group**
2. Look at **"Settlements"** section
3. **Green = they owe you** 💚
4. **Red = you owe them** ❤️

### I want to save ₹100,000 for a vacation
1. Go to **"My Dreams"** tab
2. Click **"Create Goal"**
3. Enter ₹100,000 as target
4. Set date (e.g., Dec 2025)
5. **Track progress** as you save

### I forgot my password
1. Click **"Forgot?"** on login
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new password ✓

## Voice Input Tips

### Works with:
- ✅ English: "500 for coffee"
- ✅ Hindi: "500 का कॉफी"
- ✅ Hinglish: "500 ka coffee"

### Categories auto-detected:
- 🍕 **Food**: chai, dinner, pizza, khana
- 🚗 **Travel**: petrol, uber, bus, train
- 🛍️ **Shopping**: kirana, amazon, mall, clothes
- 📱 **Bills**: rent, electricity, wifi, recharge

### If voice doesn't work:
1. Check microphone permission (click "Allow" if asked)
2. Speak clearly
3. Say amount first: "500 for food" (not "food for 500")
4. Try in a quiet place
5. Use manual entry if still stuck
